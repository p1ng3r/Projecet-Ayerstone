# Aeloria

## Lady of the Endless Horizon

Aeloria is the primary goddess of travel, sailors, navigators, explorers, Arkflight captains, risk, discovery, distant stars, and the courage to leave safe harbor.

She is not simply a sea goddess and not merely a goddess of roads. Aeloria belongs to the journey itself.

## Common Titles

- Lady of the Endless Horizon
- The First Sail
- Mistress of Fortune Winds
- Keeper of Distant Stars
- Queen of the Open Void
- The Navigator's Lantern

## Portfolio

- Travel
- Sailors
- Navigation
- Exploration
- Discovery
- Fortune
- Trade routes
- Arkflight voyages
- Risk and reward

## Domains

Primary domains include Travel, Luck, Air, Water, Wealth, and Freedom.

## Sacred Symbol

A silver-white star rising over an open horizon, often paired with a lantern, compass, or ship silhouette.

## Grand Docks

The two enormous statues above the Upper Docks are depictions of Aeloria. One is called the Lady Who Welcomes. The other is called the Lady Who Calls.

## Prayer Before Departure

> Lady of the Horizon, keep the stars before us and the void behind us.
