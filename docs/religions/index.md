# Religions of Ayerstone

Ayerstone is home to dockside shrines, noble temples, hidden cults, old household faiths, island spirits, mage chapels, and great organized religions.

This section is player-facing and should describe what ordinary citizens, sailors, priests, and adventurers commonly know.

## Current Faiths

- **Aeloria, Lady of the Endless Horizon** - goddess of travel, sailors, navigation, risk, discovery, and Arkflight voyages.

More religions will be added as they are developed.
