# Project Ayerstone

Welcome to the player-facing guide for **Ayerstone**, the Crown of the Falls.

Ayerstone is a massive floating world-shard city suspended in the dark void. Arkflight ships crowd its Grand Docks, water pours endlessly from the Waterplane Portal into Portal Lake, and the Great Houses compete for influence across its terraces, markets, temples, foundries, and chained satellite islands.

## Start Here

- Read the **Player Guide** for the setting overview.
- Explore **Districts** for Ayerstone locations.
- Use **Great Houses** to understand politics and power.
- Use **Arkflight Tech** for Arkengines, aetherite, levstone, and Ark Compasses.
