# Districts of Ayerstone

Ayerstone is built in terraces, rising roughly 150 feet from south to north. Its districts are shaped by elevation, trade, water, industry, old stone, and the needs of Arkflight.

## Core Districts

- **Grand Docks** — southwestern Arkflight harbor.
- **Merchant's Reach** — northeastern market and trade district.
- **Driftwall** — southeastern lower district.
- **Crownward** — north-center royal and governmental district.
- **Noble Row** — Great House estates around Portal Lake.
- **Waterfall Ward** — canals, riverworks, and Tidereach infrastructure.
- **Brassworks** — industry and foundries.
- **Deepstone Gate** — Ironmantle mines, shafts, and ore lifts.
