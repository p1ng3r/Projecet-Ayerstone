# Noble Row

## The Houses Above the Water

*Power lives where the Falls can be heard.*

Noble Row curves around the southern and eastern rim of Portal Lake, high above the lower city. Because Ayerstone rises roughly 150 feet from south to north, Noble Row overlooks much of the city below: the Grand Docks, Driftwall, Brassworks, the river, and the distant Chainbound Isles.

This is where the Great Houses display their wealth. Not all of it. Just enough.

## Appearance

Noble Row is broad, clean, guarded, and deliberately beautiful. Its roads are paved in pale stone. Its bridges are carved. Its terraces are lined with gardens, fountains, statues, family banners, and private watchtowers.

Cliffside villas hang over the southern drop, giving nobles a commanding view of the city and the void beyond.

## Key Features

### Portal Lake Overlook

The most prestigious stretch of Noble Row. From here, one can see the Waterplane Portal suspended above the lake, pouring endlessly into the basin below.

### House Terraces

Estate platforms belonging to the Great Houses. Each is built in its own style, from Starweaver observatory balconies to Tidereach water gardens.

### Processional Road

A ceremonial avenue connecting Noble Row to Crownward in the north and the lower city roads to the south.

### Quiet Gates

Guarded side entrances leading into private estates, old service tunnels, and noble gardens.

## Culture

Noble Row is polite warfare. Deals are made over tea. Insults are delivered through seating charts. A misplaced invitation can start a feud.

## Local Saying

> In Noble Row, even the flowers are listening.
