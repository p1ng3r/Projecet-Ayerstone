# Brassworks

## The Furnace Quarter

Brassworks is Ayerstone's industrial district: a place of foundries, machine halls, smithies, casting yards, furnace towers, coal lifts, brass pipes, and constant hammering.

House Emberhall is the dominant power here, but many smaller workshops, guild foundries, toolmakers, armorers, and machinewrights operate under its shadow.

## Appearance

The district is built from dark brick, black iron, soot-stained stone, brass rails, smokestacks, gantries, and heavy foundations. Furnace glow stains the streets orange at night.

## Major Features

### Furnace Rows

Long avenues of connected workshops and foundries.

### Casting Yards

Open industrial courts used for large metal pours, chain repair, ship fittings, anchors, and industrial components.

### Workers' Kitchens

Large communal food halls serving shift workers at all hours.

### Emberhall Forgekeep

The public seat of House Emberhall's industrial authority.

## Culture

Brassworks respects competence more than title. Nobles who cannot name the tool in their hand are treated with polite contempt.

## Local Saying

> If it exists, someone had to build it.
