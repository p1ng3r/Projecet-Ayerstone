# Waterfall Ward

Waterfall Ward follows the canals, river channels, reservoirs, and civic waterworks fed by Portal Lake.

House Tidereach holds immense influence here.

## Appearance

White stone bridges, blue tile, public fountains, water clocks, canal locks, reservoir towers, rain chains, mist gardens, water mills, and maintenance platforms define the ward. Small boats and floating work barges move constantly through the channels.

## Major Features

### Canal Locks

Systems of gates and controlled channels that guide water through the city.

### Public Reservoirs

Large civic water stores where clean water is measured and distributed.

### Waterhouses

Combination bathhouses, washhouses, tea houses, and neighborhood meeting halls.

### Tidereach Offices

Elegant but practical administrative houses where engineers, clerks, and waterkeepers manage flow charts, rights, repairs, and disputes.

## Local Saying

> Most people only notice water when it is gone.
