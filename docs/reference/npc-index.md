# NPC Index

## Ayerstone NPCs

### Aeloria
Goddess of travel, sailors, navigation, risk, and the endless horizon.

### Bilk Cheffy
Goblin owner and chef of the Gobble Inn in Driftwall.

### Bromm Cogwright
Dwarven master engineer associated with the Guild of Arkengineers.

### Celestia Starweaver
Matriarch of House Starweaver and head of the Celestial Trade and Shipping Consortium.

### Garrick Thornwall
Lord Commander of the Violet Guard.

### Grommick "Tophat" Brindletooth
Half-orc owner of the Tipsy Toad in Merchant's Reach.

### Kaelyra Vosswick
Head waitress, accountant, and practical manager of the Gobble Inn.

### Sylas Thornveil
Current hidden master of the Shadow Brotherhood, known as the Rat King.

### Tobias Finchwillow
Satyr commander of the Irystone Watch.
