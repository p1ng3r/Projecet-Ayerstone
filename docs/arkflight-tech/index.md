# Arkflight Technology

Arkflight is made possible by a blend of magic, engineering, faith, risk, and ancient materials.

Civilization survives because ships can cross the Void. Those ships rely on Arkengines, aetherite, Lifeveils, Ark Compasses, levstone systems, disciplined crews, and constant maintenance.

## Core Technologies

- **Arkengines** - distributed arcane-industrial engine systems that power void-capable ships.
- **Aetherite** - black oil-slick mineral used in cores, resonance systems, and advanced magical machinery.
- **Lifeveil** - the breathable protective envelope surrounding Arkflight vessels.
- **Levstone** - unstable lifting stone used in elevators, ship systems, cargo work, and city infrastructure.
- **Ark Compass** - navigation instrument that reads resonance, routes, currents, and jump points instead of north.
- **Void Phenomena** - the strange currents, reefs, lights, storms, and hazards encountered between worlds.

## Public Understanding

Most citizens know only the basics: Arkengines move ships, aetherite powers them, Lifeveils keep people alive, and Ark Compasses help navigators survive the Void.

Engineers know the truth is less comforting.

Every Arkflight system is a negotiated failure held together by craft, ritual, metal, magic, and people who notice problems before they become disasters.
