# House Emberhall

## Lords of Flame and Industry

House Emberhall turns raw materials into civilization. It forges anchors, chains, tools, armor, machinery, Arkengine fittings, industrial components, and the infrastructure that keeps Ayerstone working.

## Public Identity

Emberhall architecture is industrial pride: dark brick, black iron, brass accents, smokestacks, furnace towers, heavy stone foundations, iron balconies, and machine halls.

## Fashion

Emberhall fashion values durability: leather aprons, reinforced sleeves, brass buckles, heavy boots, work gloves, engraved metal jewelry, decorative tool motifs, and forge symbols.

## Ayerstone Presence

Emberhall is strongest in the Brassworks, foundries, industrial contracts, and noble forge-estates tied to manufacturing.

## Culture

Children must create something useful at the First Forge. Calluses are worn proudly. Some furnaces have burned continuously for generations.

## Common Saying

> Heat makes truth.
