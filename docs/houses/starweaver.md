## Overview

House Starweaver is the merchant house of routes, horizons, exploration, and Arkflight trade. Its power rests on shipping, jump routes, long-distance commerce, and the Celestial Trade and Shipping Consortium.

## Public Identity

Starweaver estates are built around views, observatories, balconies, map rooms, and open courtyards. Their buildings feel ready to depart. Their colors are midnight blue, silver, and white-gold.

## Fashion

Starweaver fashion favors long coats, layered fabrics, silver embroidery, compass motifs, star patterns, flowing scarves, hidden map pockets, and jewelry shaped like navigation tools.

## Ayerstone Presence

Starweaver power is most visible in Noble Row, the Grand Docks, and the Celestial Trade and Shipping Consortium on the Upper Docks.

## Culture

Children create their first map before adulthood. Captains keep blue lanterns lit from old ceremonial flames. Guests are often asked where they would go if given one free voyage.

## Common Saying

> The route exists. You simply have not found it.
