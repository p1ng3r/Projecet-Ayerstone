# House Blackwake

## Lords of the Black Wake

House Blackwake is the house of privateers, convoy escorts, anti-piracy contracts, naval discipline, and hard-earned protection.

## Public Identity

Blackwake estates resemble naval fortresses: black stone walls, weathered timber, brass lanterns, watch towers, signal beacons, armories, and captured pirate banners.

## Fashion

Blackwake nobles favor naval coats, high boots, brass buttons, dark leather, weatherproof cloaks, medals, battle ribbons, and ship insignia.

## Ayerstone Presence

Blackwake power is strongest around the Grand Docks, convoy offices, escort halls, and Noble Row holdings overlooking the harbor.

## Culture

Every child serves a ceremonial First Watch. Family feasts leave one chair empty for sailors who never returned.

## Common Saying

> Storms reveal captains.
