## Overview

House Stormglass designs, builds, tests, and repairs Arkflight ships. It is the house of shipwrights, engineers, sail architects, hull mathematicians, prototype pilots, and dangerous dreamers.

## Public Identity

Stormglass estates contain drafting rooms, model halls, private forges, sail lofts, wind chambers, testing balconies, and miniature drydocks. Their colors are storm-gray, copper, and blue glass.

## Fashion

Stormglass nobles dress like they may need to repair something suddenly: rolled sleeves, tool belts, reinforced coats, copper buttons, blue glass jewelry, fingerless gloves, and practical boots.

## Ayerstone Presence

Stormglass influence is strongest at the Grand Docks, shipyards, drydocks, Noble Row, and experimental platforms near the city edge.

## Culture

Children receive a First Hammer and are expected to make something useful before adulthood. Failed inventions are preserved as lessons.

## Common Saying

> The void does not forgive sloppy work.
