# House Aurelian

## The Crown Upon the Stone

House Aurelian is the royal house of Ayerstone. Where other houses measure power in ships, contracts, ore, or engines, Aurelian measures it in continuity.

## Public Identity

Aurelian architecture is white marble, gold trim, violet banners, grand stairs, reflecting pools, palace gardens, and monumental statuary. Their buildings feel designed to outlive everyone who visits them.

## Fashion

Aurelian formal dress uses white fabrics, gold trim, violet accents, embroidered crowns, and floating-stone motifs. Royal styles often remain unchanged for generations.

## Ayerstone Presence

House Aurelian rules from Crownward Palace in Crownward, north-center above Noble Row and Portal Lake.

## Culture

Royal children are taught duty through ceremony, history, etiquette, and public visibility. The Water Oath, Hall of Kings vigils, and Founder's Feast shape family identity.

## Common Saying

> The crown is not worn. It is carried.
