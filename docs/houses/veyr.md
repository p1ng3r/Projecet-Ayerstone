## Overview

House Veyr is the house of banking, contracts, insurance, debt, inheritance, investment, and quiet financial power.

## Public Identity

Veyr architecture is measured and deliberate: ivory stone, dark green glass, black marble, polished brass, geometric patterns, mirrored halls, and silent vaults.

## Fashion

Veyr fashion is subtle wealth: emerald accents, silver thread, black velvet, ivory fabrics, ledger-chain jewelry, and formal restraint.

## Ayerstone Presence

House Veyr is strongest in Merchant's Reach, counting houses, financial courts, and Noble Row estates.

## Culture

Veyr children learn signatures, law, finance, contracts, and consequence early. Important contracts are read three times: for legality, intent, and consequence.

## Common Saying

> Gold spends. Reputation compounds.

## Court Whispers

!!! info "Public Rumor"
    A Veyr clerk can read a family fortune from the handwriting on a single receipt.

!!! warning "Risky Rumor"
    Some debts in Veyr ledgers are inherited by people who have never heard of the original contract.

!!! question "Whispered Claim"
    The oldest Veyr vault may contain sealed accounts tied to vanished noble lines.
