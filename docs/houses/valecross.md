# House Valecross

## Keepers of Green Breath

House Valecross is the house of agriculture, Lifeveil ecology, food security, gardens, fey diplomacy, living wards, and the green systems that help Ayerstone breathe.

## Public Identity

Valecross estates are botanical compounds of cream stone, green terraces, bronze rails, glowing flowers, living walls, fey doors, orchards, and hidden garden courts.

## Fashion

Valecross fashion uses greens, creams, bronze seed jewelry, embroidered leaves, living flowers, soft fabrics, garden cloaks, and practical outdoor boots.

## Ayerstone Presence

Valecross is visible in Noble Row, agricultural holdings, Lifeveil gardens, market food halls, and the greener Chainbound Isles.

## Culture

Children learn planting, weather signs, seasonal rites, and respect for small fey neighbors. Pixies and fairies are common in Valecross spaces.

## Common Saying

> A city breathes because something grows.
