## Overview

House Ironmantle controls mining, stonework, deep shafts, ore claims, aetherite extraction, and the hidden foundations of Ayerstone.

## Public Identity

Ironmantle buildings favor black granite, iron supports, thick columns, vaulted ceilings, brass fixtures, and exposed support beams. Many estates are partly subterranean.

## Fashion

Ironmantles favor heavy coats, leather gloves, reinforced boots, metal jewelry, practical belts, iron thread, stone clasps, and ore gemstones.

## Ayerstone Presence

Their public face sits near the Grand Docks and Deepstone Gate, with ore lifts, cranes, levstone elevators, and vertical shafts descending into the heart of Ayerstone.

## Culture

Ironmantle children receive toy hammers, learn ore by smell, and keep the stone fragment from their First Strike ceremony for life.

## Common Saying

> The stone remembers.
