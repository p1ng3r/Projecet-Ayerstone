# Ayerstone Atlas

## Orientation

Ayerstone rises approximately 150 feet from south to north.

- Southwest: Grand Docks
- Northeast: Merchant's Reach
- Southeast: Driftwall
- North-Center: Crownward
- Around Portal Lake: Noble Row

## Portal Lake

At the center of the city lies Portal Lake, roughly two miles across.

Above it hangs the Waterplane Portal, a massive blue-white vortex pouring water continuously into the lake.

## Major Districts

### Grand Docks
The gateway to Arkflight civilization.

### Merchant's Reach
The commercial heart of the city.

### Driftwall
The crowded southeastern district.

### Crownward
Royal authority and government.

### Noble Row
The estates of the Great Houses.

### Brassworks
Industry and foundries.

### Deepstone Gate
Mining and extraction.

### Waterfall Ward
Canals and civic waterworks.

## The Chainbound Isles

The outer islands orbit the life of Ayerstone through trade, resources, culture, and travel.
